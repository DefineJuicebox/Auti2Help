About us

A Few Words about Auti2Help

Our journey began with a simple yet profound realization: that the traditional job market often overlooks the unique talents and abilities of people with autism. We decided to become a part of the solution, creating a platform where individuals with autism can not only find suitable job opportunities but also receive the mentorship and support they need to thrive in their chosen fields.

At Auti2Help, we pair individuals with experienced mentors who understand the nuances of autism. These mentors work closely with each person to discover their strengths, passions, and ideal career paths. We believe that a deep understanding of each individualâ€™s capabilities is key to guiding them towards fulfilling employment opportunities.

We are more than just a service; we are a community that advocates for the value and inclusion of autistic individuals in the workforce. Our goal is to create a world where autism is not seen as a barrier to employment, but as a unique perspective that can contribute to the richness and diversity of the professional world.
